About Us

Our Story

The name Norton-Gauss encapsulates our mission.

Inspired by the Norton theorem, which simplifies complex linear circuits, and the Gauss theorem, which bridges external forces and internal fields, our purpose is clear: simplifying customer challenges by bringing external expertise, innovative solutions, and cutting-edge knowledge.


We partner with SMEs across industries, transforming their operations with tailored solutions designed to foster growth, efficiency, and lasting success.
