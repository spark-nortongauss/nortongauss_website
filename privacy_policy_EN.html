Privacy Policy

    Your Data. Our Commitment.

Introduction

At Norton-Gauss, your privacy is our top priority. We are dedicated to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with global regulations such as the GDPR (Europe), LGPD (Brazil), and CCPA (United States).

Information We Collect

We collect personal data to provide better services and fulfill legal obligations. This includes:

  • Identifiable Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and usage data.

This information is collected when you visit our website, interact with us, or use our services.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To deliver and improve our services.
  • To communicate updates, offers, and news.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • To enhance user experience through analytics.

Your Rights

You have the following rights under GDPR, LGPD, and CCPA:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to file a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Data Security

We use robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and access control policies. While no system is 100% secure, we continuously update our practices to mitigate risks.
